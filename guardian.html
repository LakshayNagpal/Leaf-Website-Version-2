How "SAFER" Works?

Safer works on tapping principle and sends instant alerts to your friends, family, the police and the volunteers of the Safer community in the proximity to inform them about your location.

Prompt notifications are received by the concerned on their mobile phones and your location can be easily tracked by the Safer mobile application that traces Safer’s locale and immediately guides the aide to you.

Safer has an embedded Low energy Bluetooth V4.0 module which pairs itself with your mobile handset. The device comes with an amazingly long battery life of six months.

Safer cares for you and does not disturb you by vibrating in order to notify. It works on the Haptic Feedback Mechanism and also comes with a feature of Anti-lost where it notifies you in case you are forgetting your phone.
